Bora La - Auto Cortes

Private creator automation tool for publishing vertical short videos.

About this app

Bora La is a private automation tool used by the owner of the connected TikTok account to publish vertical short videos generated from their own content workflow.

The app is not available to the public, does not sell services, and does not allow external users to log in.

How it works

  • The owner authorizes the app using TikTok Login Kit.
  • The app stores the authorization token on the owner's private server.
  • The editing pipeline generates vertical MP4 videos locally.
  • Eligible videos are uploaded or published to the authorized TikTok account.
