Bora La is a private creator automation tool used to publish vertical short videos to a TikTok account authorized by the account owner.

Use of the app

This app is restricted to the owner of the connected TikTok account. It is not a public service and does not provide access to external users.

Content responsibility

The account owner is responsible for all videos, captions, hashtags and other content published through the app.

Automation workflow

The app may upload or publish MP4 vertical short videos generated locally by the owner's editing pipeline.

Termination

The owner may stop using the app at any time by removing local tokens, disconnecting the app, or revoking TikTok permissions.
