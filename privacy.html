Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Bora La is a private automation tool used by the owner of the connected TikTok account to upload or publish vertical short videos through TikTok's official API.

Data accessed

The app may access basic account information required for authentication, such as the authorized account identifier and display information provided by TikTok Login Kit.

Tokens

Access tokens and refresh tokens are stored locally on the owner's private server and are used only to upload or publish videos to the authorized TikTok account.

Content

The app processes MP4 videos generated by the owner's local editing pipeline. The app does not collect content from public users.

Data sharing

The app does not sell, rent or share personal data with third parties.

Removal

The account owner may remove stored tokens or revoke the app's TikTok permissions at any time.
